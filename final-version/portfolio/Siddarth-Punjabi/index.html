Siddarth Punjabi

He has 4.5 years of experience across various facets in Financial Services and in all spheres of Front, Middle and Back office. Prior to joining Pecan, he has worked with companies such as AdviseSure Ventures Pvt. Ltd. one of India’s first Robo-Advisor, as Market and Equity Research Analyst, Axis Securities which was part of the Axis Bank Group and eClerx in the Knowledge Management Team; among others. He is a CFA level 2 Candidate and holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Financial Markets from Mumbai University.
