About Company

Pecan REAMS was formed with an intention to help people manage Real Estate Assets efficiently. Real estate assets comprises of nearly 25-50% of an individual’s asset portfolio. However there are no tools which help manage real estate efficiently.

REAMS is a platform providing high quality Asset Management Services focusing on cost savings, time management and efficient real estate strategy. Founded by an industry veteran along with an experienced team of professionals, REAMS has been successful in discovering opportunities for clients and efficiently managing their portfolio. Providing end to end services, REAMS wants to make managing real estate truly hassle-free process.

The dedicated team of professionals with a mix of dynamic young and talented experienced professionals is constantly striving towards making the lives of our clients and business associates easier.
