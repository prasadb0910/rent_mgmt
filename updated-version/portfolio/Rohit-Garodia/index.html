Rohit Garodia

Founder & Managing Director

Head – Real Estate & Alternative Asset Management

  • Rohit has over 10 years combined experience in real estate investments, commercial and investment banking, and early stage start- up companies.
  • Prior to starting Pecan, he was Director and Co-Head – Real Estate for JM Financial, one of India’s largest financial services player.
    • Responsible for the Real Estate business including investing, lending & advisory services and served on board of portfolio companies.
    • He has detailed knowledge of all major real estate markets in India and of real estate rules & regulations.
  • Earlier he was in the Investment Banking group at Morgan Stanley in the Corporate Finance coverage group. Select transactions include:
    • US$58 million investment into Mantri Developers, first entity level FDI in real estate in India.
    • US$20 billion demerger of Reliance Industries Limited.
    • US$2.4 billion Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Offer for Sale: Largest Capital Market Offering in Indian History.
    • US$ 20 million investment in Times of India (India’s largest Media house) News Channel by Reuters.
  • He has also worked with ICICI Bank in their Corporate Banking Group.
  • He is also actively involved in angel investing and has interests in a large executive search company, a facilities management company and a financial news service provider.
  • Rohit is an Associate Chartered Accountant from Institute of Chartered Accountants of India where he was ranked among the top 1% of his graduating class and he is did his undergrad from Narsee Monjee College, Mumbai.
