About Company

At REAMS, Real Estate Asset Management comes naturally given our combined rich experience of Decades. We aim to provide high quality Asset Management Services which strong focus on quality control and risk mitigation. Real Estate Assets in India constitute nearly 1/3rd the total Assets.

Given the huge opportunity, we have been successful in discovering opportunities for our clients and efficacious in capitalizing them. We deliver Value Addition for the long run for clients with representation for Transaction management, Documentation & Asset Management.
